<About/>

The Creative Computing Society of Thapar University, commonly known as CCS, has been founded with the vision of nurturing and fostering the youth generation and creating awareness about the reigning era of technology. The student run committee tirelessly works to achieve it's ambition of bridging the Gap between students and computer technology and science. As a proud group we not only want to enrich the minds, but rather aims to enable a healthy understanding of the subject to the students of Thapar University. CCS is pledged and devoted to provide a strong rostrum for all the budding and aspiring technocrats, so that they can make a difference and fulfil our aim to make mankind a developed, prosperous and indeed a better place to live in.
